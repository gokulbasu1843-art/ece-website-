👨‍💻 Charles Babbage

Known as the Father of Computer.

📘 Introduction

The word Computer comes from the Latin word Compute, which means to calculate.

The first computer concept called the Analytical Engine was invented by Charles Babbage in 1837.

Analytical Engine by Charles Babbage

🖥️ What is Computer?

A Computer is an electronic programmable device that accepts data as input, processes it and gives output as information.

Desktop Computer System

✅ Advantages of Computer

  • ⚡ High Speed
  • 🎯 Accuracy
  • 💾 Large Storage
  • 🤖 Automation
  • 🔁 Diligence
  • 🔄 Versatility
  • 💰 Cost Effective

❌ Disadvantages of Computer

  • Needs regular maintenance

🕰️ Generations of Computer

Five Generations of Computer

1️⃣ First Generation

Technology: Vacuum Tubes

Features: Large size, high heat and electricity use.

2️⃣ Second Generation

Technology: Transistors

Features: Smaller, faster and reliable.

3️⃣ Third Generation

Technology: Integrated Circuits

Features: Faster speed and lower power use.

4️⃣ Fourth Generation

Technology: Microprocessor

Features: GUI, networking and portable PCs.

5️⃣ Fifth Generation

Technology: Artificial Intelligence

Features: Smart computers and automation.

🌍 Uses of Computer

Uses of Computer in Education, Business, Banking, Hospital and Entertainment

Computers are used in every field to make work easier, faster and more accurate.

🏫 Education

Used in schools and colleges for online classes, smart learning and projects.

🏥 Hospital

Doctors use computers for reports, patient records and medical research.

🏦 Banking

Used in ATM services, online banking and money transfer systems.

🎮 Entertainment

Computers are used for games, movies, music and video editing.

🏢 Business

Used in offices for billing, accounting and communication.

🚀 Science & Research

Scientists use computers for space research, weather forecasting and simulations.

💡 Computer Quote
